<h3>Gamemode help</h3>
<pre>
Welcome to the Hacker vs Hacker server!

This gamemode is a round-based teamplay in the style of 
Counter-Strike: Source where cheaters play against each other.

Press F1 to open the MOTD panel.
Press F2 to open the team selection menu.
Press F4 to open the weapon selection menu.
</pre>
<h3>Chat commands</h3>
<pre>
!nextmap
 - Show the next map.

!currentmap
 - Show the current map.

!thetime
 - Show current server time.

!timeleft
 - Show remaining time on map.

!rank [name | steamid], !stats [name | steamid]
 - Show player statistics.

!top, !top10
 - Open the menu with top players.

!votemap
 - Vote for a map change.

!motd, !help
 - Open the MOTD panel.

!guns
 - Open the weapon selection menu.

!team
 - Open the team selection menu.

!t, !ter
 - Join the Terrorist team.

!ct
 - Join the Counter-Terrorist team.

!spec, !spectate
 - Join the spectators.

!rs, !resetscore
 - Reset your frags and deaths.

!ak47, !deagle, etc.
 - Get a weapon by its name.
</pre>
